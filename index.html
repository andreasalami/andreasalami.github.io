Bio

Traveling fuels my curiosity, I love exploring new cultures and landscapes, whether solo or with good company. Sports have always been a key part of my life; I’ve played football competitively and enjoy running, cycling, skiing, and trekking.

I'm deeply committed to sustainability, constantly seeking ways to minimise my environmental footprint and reduce pollution. I stay informed about politics and financial markets, and I have a strong passion for history and philosophy, subjects I explored in depth during my high school years.

As a tech enthusiast, I have expertise in both hardware and software. Whether working on Windows or macOS, I navigate seamlessly between systems. Through my diverse experiences and interests, I strive to make a meaningful impact, both in my community and beyond.
